About Me

I'm Pavel Todica, a passionate developer and project manager with extensive experience in the gaming industry, particularly in the FiveM roleplay community. My journey began as a Lead Developer at Melilla Titans, where I honed my skills in game development and community management.

I'm currently pursuing a degree in Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology (ETTI) at the "Gheorghe Asachi" Technical University of Iași, with a strong focus on embedded systems and cybersecurity. I'm deeply passionate about modern security technologies, ethical hacking, and the challenges of protecting digital environments.

Today, I serve as the CEO of Kyxel Studios, the innovative company behind Kyxel Security and Coimoi eSports. Our mission is to create cutting-edge security solutions and deliver exceptional gaming experiences to players worldwide.
